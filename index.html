Environmental & Public Health

Compostify

Divert organic waste from landfills through convenient collection, education, and data-driven partnerships.

Mission & purpose

Compostify closes the loop on campus by capturing food and yard waste, composting it responsibly, and returning nutrients to local soils — reducing methane emissions and improving community resilience.

Waste isn’t just environmental — it’s public health: air quality, pests, heat resilience, and food security.

Plan of action
  • Start with park cleanups (Aggie Park → Lake Bryan and beyond)
  • Pilot compost bins in dining halls and the MSC (with approvals)
  • Guest speakers (EPA / School of Public Health)
  • Community expansion to parks and high-traffic areas
  • Awareness campaigns + contamination-reduction education
KPIs: lbs diverted • methane avoided • contamination rate • soil returned

Research & Advocacy

HPO Initiative Chapter

Train future healthcare leaders to critically engage with discoveries, write professionally, and advocate evidence-based progress.

What it is

“Vital Discoveries TAMU” is a student writing platform + workshop ecosystem that turns academic curiosity into journal-ready communication and credible contributions.

Plan of action
  1. Student writing platform: publish research summaries/op-eds (goal: 6 pieces first semester).
  2. Writing & research workshops: at least 3 per academic year.
  3. Peer mentorship: mentor-mentee pairs for feedback cycles (TBD).
  4. Research collaboration & publication: annual submission (stretch goal).
  5. Advocacy events & seminars: one major event each semester.

AI • Data • Responsible Tech

AI / Machine Learning Chapter

Apply AI/ML to healthcare and public health with a strong focus on ethics, privacy, bias, and equity.

How it fits

The AI/ML Chapter connects all AHC sectors by building tools (dashboards, models, apps) that amplify impact and improve accessibility — without compromising ethics or privacy.

Plan of action
  • AI/ML workshops (Python, R, TensorFlow, health datasets)
  • Cross-sector projects (e.g., Compostify prediction + HPO text-mining tools)
  • Hackathons and prototyping nights
  • AI ethics & policy forum (consent, privacy, bias)
  • Outreach products (dashboards/apps) each semester
